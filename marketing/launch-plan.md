# Product Hunt Launch Plan (7-day)

**T-7**: Finalize landing page, pricing, and screenshots. DM friendly supporters.  
**T-5**: Tease on LinkedIn + X (no link).  
**T-3**: Post preview video (30s) of Kanban + AI cover letter.  
**T-2**: Soft-invite waitlist. Prepare first comment copy.  
**T-1**: Schedule launch for 00:01 PST.  
**Launch Day**:
- Post: Title, tagline, 5 screenshots, short video
- First comment: Founder story + roadmap
- Reply to every comment
- Share to LinkedIn/Reddit/IndieHackers (once, human tone)
**Post-Day**:
- Email new users: thanks + onboarding Day 1
