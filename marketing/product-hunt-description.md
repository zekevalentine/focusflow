# Job Hunter FocusFlow — Land your next role faster

**What it does**  
Kanban for your job hunt + AI tools (cover letters, daily plan, interview coach).

**Why now**  
Hiring is noisy. Focus and consistency win. FocusFlow keeps you organized and helps you write better, faster.

**Who it's for**  
Students, laid-off folks, and career switchers in tech, design, marketing, sales.

**Key features**  
- Drag-and-drop Kanban for applications  
- AI cover letters tailored to each JD  
- Daily Focus planner to stay consistent  
- Interview Coach (targeted questions + guidance)  
- Analytics: interview & offer rates
